Computing emphasizes forward vs inverse modeling

What is the interest rate if an investment doubles in five years?

 
$$ \hbox{School: }\;\;\; A = A_0 \left(1+ \frac{p}{100}\right)^n $$

 

Fundamental model:

 
$$ A_{n+1} = A_n + A_n \frac{p_n}{100}, \quad A_0 \hbox{ given} $$

 

  • Input: \( p \), \( A_0 \)

  • Output: \( A_1,\ldots,A_N \)

Computing frees time for more focus on problem formulation and results

With computing complex is often simpler

 
$$ \ddot\theta (t) + \omega^2\sin(\theta(t)) = 0 $$

 

Original/fundamental model (DAE):

 
$$ \begin{align*} m\,\ddot{\boldsymbol{r}} &= \boldsymbol{F} \\ ||\boldsymbol{r}|| &= \hbox{const} \end{align*} $$

 

Parts of \( \boldsymbol{F} \) unknown, parts of \( \boldsymbol{r} \) known.

Deformable rope gives a clean Newton's 2nd law:

 
$$ m\ddot{\boldsymbol{r}} = \boldsymbol{F}(\boldsymbol{r}, \dot{\boldsymbol{r}}) $$
